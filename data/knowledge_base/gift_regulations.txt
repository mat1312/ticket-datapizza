=== NORTH POLE GIFT REGULATIONS (2025 Edition) ===

PROHIBITED ITEMS (DO NOT PACK):
- Uranium or Radioactive Materials (Refer to HazMat Protocol).
- Live Animals (Unless equipped with dietary plan and air holes).
- Cash (> $50 equivalent).
- Weapons (Real). Toy versions must have orange tips.
- Cursed Artifacts.

RESTRICTED ITEMS (REQUIRES PARENTAL CONSENT FORM):
- Drum Kits (Noise Level Class 5).
- Slime/Goo Kits (> 1 Gallon).
- Pets (Dragons, Unicorns).
- Chemistry Sets (Explosive Grade).

SUBSTITUTION RULES:
- If Stock = 0: Substitute with nearest category equivalent (e.g., Doll -> Action Figure).
- If Naughty Score > 50: Mandatory Substitution -> COAL (Standard Lump).
- If Naughty Score > 80: High-Grade Anthracite Coal + Warning Note.

PACKAGING STANDARDS:
- Bows: Must be perfectly symmetrical.
- Paper: 100% Recyclable Magic Paper.
- Tags: Must match Child ID from Database exactly.
