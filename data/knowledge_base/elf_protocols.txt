=== ELF WORKER PROTOCOLS (E.L.F. Union Approved) ===

WORK SCHEDULE & BREAKS:
- Shift Duration: 4 hours on, 1 hour off (Cookie Break).
- Overtime: Only authorized by Head Elf (paid in double hot cocoa).
- Naps: Mandatory 20-minute power nap every 6 hours.

SAFETY REGULATIONS:
- Toy Testing: All toys must be tested in the "Bouncy Room" before packing.
- Sharp Objects: Pointy ears protection required in Workshop Sector 4.
- Glitter Hazard: Maximum exposure 2 hours/day without shielding.
- Reindeer Zone: Unauthorized elves keep 10ft distance from antlers.

EMERGENCY CODES:
- CODE RED: Santa Suit Ripped.
- CODE BLUE: Toy Production halted.
- CODE GREEN: Elf stuck in wrapping paper machine.
- CODE YELLOW: Reindeer loose in the cafeteria.

REPORTING:
- Strikes: Must be filed 24h in advance with the Union Rep (Blinky).
- Grievances: Submit to "Official Complaint Box" (Sector 7G).
